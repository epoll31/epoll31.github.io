Section 1: Stress

In this section I created a program to simulate Stress.

Press Esc to clear the screen.

Press Backspace to return to the previous page.

Stress is something that everybody has to deal with at some time or another. Managing that stress can be really hard for some people. I know thatI definetely find it hard to manage quite often. I made this simulation to help explain how stress can build up over time. Specifically this shows how with every new task or chore comes more tasks and chores. I demonstrate a task o hcore as a button on the screen. If you want to complete a task you must click it to erase it. However, this creates a few more buttons that you must remove. This keeps spiraling until you have a mess of buttons on your screen which emulates letting multiple tasks build up and not knowing where to begin to complete them.
